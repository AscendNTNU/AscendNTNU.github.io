Technical groups in Ascend

AI

Finding the optimal behaviour to solve the mission at hand. The Artificial Intelligence group takes the latest AI research from theory to practice.

Control

The control group takes care of all the physical aspects of the drone. Bridging the gap between commands and actions.

Sensory

State estimation of the drone and beyond. The sensory group converts sensor input to meaningful information about the drone and its environment.
